dosgToggleObjectsKeyboardControl:

  Each object uses a modifier key to specify what under node the toggle
  object should be placed

    'w' or 'W'		under the "world" node
    'e' or 'E'		under the "ether" node
    's' or 'S'		under the "scene" node
    'n' or 'N'		under the "nav" node

  The objects are:
    'c' or 'C'		cube- four presses to cycle through frame and panel
    'f' or 'F'		frame
    'g' or 'G'		gnomon
    'a' or 'A'		axis
    't' or 'T'		text label

  'Backspace' turns off all toggle objects
